Photo club Rules

A good functioning photo club needs a set of rules to stay productive and organized. Only Album art, Novel cover art, and Video game cover art will be approved, any other submissions will not be approved. Any submissions with extremely graphic content, or sexual content will not be accepted, and the IP address of the person submitting will be flagged for future submissions.
