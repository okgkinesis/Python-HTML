We Pride in our Community

We love to see the content our community brings out, and thats why we owe you a great thanks, otherwise this photoclub would never exist. so wed like to take a moment to talk about the contributing members of the group.

Mary

A small town teacher, Mary has been contributing to the photoclub for ten years now, only to have won her first submission just the other year. Mary loves her dog Marty, and her grandfather Eustace.

Tony

Tony has been going to school for aerospace engineering for the past 5 years, and will be earning his masters in molecular physics, but still finds time to post to our photo club. He hopes to change the way we handle physics in aerospace vehicles.

Bobby

Bobby has been a fireman for 10 years, and he says he loves every minute of his time at the station. He has no family so he enjoys the company of his fellow firemen. When Bobby isnt waiting on staff at the station, he enjoys hiking, and other outdoor activities.

Andy

Andy is a 90 year old retiree who still has the wits to understand most of the chat rooms jokes. He doesnt find too many photos on the internet to submit, but he can care to open up the chat room page.
